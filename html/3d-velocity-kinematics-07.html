Manipulability

Manipulability is defined as

\[ m=\frac{\min R}{\max R}, 0 \leq m \leq 1\]

where

  • \(m=1\) : isotropic motion
  • \(m=0\) : the ellipsoid is an ellipse, motion in one Cartesian direction is not possible
